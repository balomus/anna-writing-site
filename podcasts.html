Turning to Story: A Writing Podcast

Authors Lyssa Mia Smith and Anna Mercier cohost Turning To Story a, a craft-focused podcast and weekly newsletter for fiction writers at all levels. The goal of Turning to Story is to help fellow writers keep the joy in writing by focusing on the one thing on the publishing roller coaster they can control: the quality of their stories. Content is presented in a light-hearted, conversational format. New episodes are released weekly and feature a deep dive into a specific craft technique using examples from bestsellers and fan favorites. Turning to Story's Substack contains archives of their newsletter, and is chock full of free writing resources. The podcast is available for free across platforms. Come for the craft; stay for the laughs!
